Failure to discipline established misconduct is the departmental refusal to impose internal consequences on officers whose misconduct has been documented or adjudicated. Concrete forms include closed cases despite sustained complaints, transfers in lieu of discipline, the rehiring by other departments of officers fired for misconduct elsewhere, and contract provisions that delete sustained findings after specified intervals. The publication tracks documented patterns of non-discipline.
