Federal deployment against civilians is the use of federal armed forces, federalized National Guard, or federal law enforcement in coercive roles against US civilians — protest policing, immigration enforcement in interior cities, response to civil unrest — in ways that exceed the lawful frameworks for such deployment or that use those frameworks pretextually. The publication tracks documented deployments and the legal authorities invoked, applying the standard symmetrically across administrations.
