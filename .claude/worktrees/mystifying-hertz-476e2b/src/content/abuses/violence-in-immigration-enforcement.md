Violence in immigration enforcement is excessive force, abusive conditions, or coercive practice in immigration custody, removal, and enforcement operations. Concrete forms include force used in apprehension that meets the excessive-force standard, conditions of detention that produce avoidable harm, sexual abuse and assault in detention, and operational tactics designed to coerce signed waivers or coerce compliance with removal proceedings.
