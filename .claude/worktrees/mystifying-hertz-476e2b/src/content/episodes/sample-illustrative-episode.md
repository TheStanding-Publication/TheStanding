This is an illustrative sample episode used during scaffolding to demonstrate the episode taxonomy. It does not document a real event.

The body of an episode is its editorial overview — what was the compound real-world happening, what was its scale and duration, what was the broader context that ties the individual recorded entries together. The specific abuses are documented in their own entries; this is the connective tissue that lets readers see them as parts of one episode rather than scattered incidents.

When real episodes are filed, this sample should be deleted alongside the [sample illustrative entry](/entries/2026/05/10/sample-illustrative-entry/) that references it.
