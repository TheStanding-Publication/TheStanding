# How to Submit Tips

The Standing accepts tips and corrections from the public about anti-democratic actions and institutional abuses. Here's how to submit:

## Submission channels

- **Email:** [tips@thestanding.us](mailto:tips@thestanding.us) (anonymous-friendly)
- **GitHub:** [Open an issue on our public repository](https://github.com/TheStanding-Publication/TheStanding/issues)

## What we accept

- Information sourced entirely from publicly available material (news articles, public records, official statements, video, etc.)
- Corrections to existing entries
- Examples of anti-democratic actions or abuses that fit our taxonomy

## What we don't accept

- Attachments or confidential documents
- Whistleblower information or leaked materials
- Rumors or unverified claims
- Information available only through private channels

## Important

All tips become public GitHub issues. We work transparently—your submission (minus any identifying details you want removed) will be publicly visible as we research and verify it.
