A federal court barred ICE from making civil arrests at three Manhattan immigration courthouses, finding the agency lacked internal legal authority for the practice. The next day, ICE arrested a man at a Manhattan immigration court anyway. The pair captures a single confrontation between a court order and the agency's open defiance of it.
