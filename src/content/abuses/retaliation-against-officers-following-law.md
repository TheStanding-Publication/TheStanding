Retaliation against officers following the law — military or federal law enforcement personnel who refuse unlawful orders, who report misconduct up the chain, or who testify truthfully under oath — corrodes the lawful chain of command and signals that loyalty to person, not law, is the operative norm. Concrete forms include reassignment to punitive postings, blocked promotions, security-clearance revocations, and public denunciation. The publication tracks documented retaliation linked to the officer's compliance with law or duty.
